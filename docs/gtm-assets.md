# GTM assets — demo scripts + client #1 free-channel

Concrete, ready-to-use assets for the launch window. House rules baked in:
**no phone calls** (every CTA = reply / "I'll send a short example"), brand =
**Opero**, plain language (no "automation / workflow / AI agent" jargon), UK
English, niche = **dental + allied-health clinics**. No hard price figures in
first touch; if asked, "a small one-off to set up + a low monthly to run it".

---

## A. Demo videos (the open loop the cold-email CTA promises)

The cold email ends with "reply and I'll send a short example of how it'd work
for you." These are those examples — **two 30–45s screen recordings**, no face,
no voice needed (on-screen captions are fine). Record once, reuse per reply.
Keep them generic enough to send to any clinic; personalise only the clinic
name in the reply message, not the video.

### Demo 1 — Missed-call text-back (the flagship; send to almost everyone)

> **Goal of the clip:** show a missed call instantly turning into a booked
> patient, with zero staff effort.

**Shot list (screen recording on a phone):**
1. (0–5s) Caption: *"A patient calls your clinic. Reception's busy / it's after
   hours."* Show a phone ringing, then "Missed call".
2. (5–18s) Caption: *"Within seconds, they get a text back — automatically."*
   Show an SMS arriving:
   > "Hi, this is {Clinic} — sorry we missed your call! Did you want to book an
   > appointment or ask a question? Reply here and we'll sort it out."
3. (18–32s) Show the patient replying "Yes, need an appointment for a filling",
   and a friendly automated back-and-forth offering two slots.
4. (32–42s) Caption: *"Booked — without anyone picking up. The call you'd have
   lost is now a patient."* Show the slot confirmed.
5. End card: **Opero · opero-studio.com**

**Reply message to attach it to:**
> Hi {Clinic} team — here's the 40-second example I mentioned. This is the
> missed-call text-back: when you can't pick up, the caller gets a text straight
> away and can book from their phone, so they don't just ring the next practice.
> Want me to set up a version with your clinic's name and booking link so you can
> try it? — Opero · opero-studio.com

### Demo 2 — Instant web-enquiry reply (for clinics with a contact form)

> **Goal:** show a website enquiry getting an instant, helpful reply instead of
> sitting unanswered for hours.

**Shot list:**
1. (0–6s) Caption: *"Someone fills in your website contact form at 9pm."*
2. (6–20s) Caption: *"They get a reply in under a minute — not next morning."*
   Show an email/SMS: "Thanks for getting in touch with {Clinic}! To book you in
   faster, which day works best..."
3. (20–34s) Show a short exchange that captures what they need + offers a slot.
4. (34–42s) Caption: *"Enquiries answered while you sleep. Fewer lost to the
   clinic that replied first."* End card: **Opero · opero-studio.com**

**Reply message:** same shape as Demo 1, swap "missed-call text-back" for
"instant reply to website enquiries".

## B. Client #1 from free channels (no warmup needed — start today)

The first client should come from a channel that needs **no domain warmup**:
manual Gmail, Instagram/LinkedIn DM, or a personal intro. The hook is a **free
setup** for the first 1–2 clinics (a case study is worth more than the fee).

### B1. Instagram / Facebook DM (good for clinics active on social)

> Hi! Quick one — I help {city} clinics stop losing patients to missed calls and
> slow replies. I'm setting up a couple of local practices for free to build some
> local examples. It texts back every missed call so the patient books with you
> instead of ringing the next place. Want me to send a 40-sec example for {Clinic}?

If they reply → send **Demo 1** + this:
> Happy to set it up for {Clinic} at no cost — I just ask to use the result as a
> small example for other clinics. No call needed, I can do it all over message.
> Want me to start?

### B2. Manual Gmail (personal, one at a time — not bulk)

> **Subject:** missed calls at {Clinic}
>
> Hi {Clinic} team,
>
> When the phone goes unanswered — lunch, after hours, mid-treatment — that
> caller usually just rings the next practice. I set up a thing for a couple of
> local clinics that instantly texts back every missed call, so the patient books
> with you instead.
>
> I'm doing the setup free for the first couple of practices to build local
> examples. Want me to send a 40-second example of how it'd look for {Clinic}?
>
> — Opero · opero-studio.com
>
> *Not relevant? Just reply "no" and I won't follow up.*

### B3. The free-setup offer (framing)

- **What they get:** missed-call text-back set up and running on their number,
  free, in exchange for permission to use the result as a small example/case
  study. No lock-in.
- **Why free for #1–2:** a real clinic result unblocks every later pitch (the
  GTM content blocker). One case study compounds.
- **The ask is always a reply or "I'll set it up" — never a call.**
- After it's live and they've seen value, the paid version (more channels:
  web-enquiry replies, reminders, reactivation) is the natural next step.
